Privacy Policy for K-SimpleQR

Last updated: 15 July 2025

K-SimpleQR ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect and use information in our app.

Information Collection and Use

Advertising: We use Google AdMob to display ads. Google may collect device and usage data to personalize and measure ads. No personal data is collected directly by us.

Subscriptions: K-SimpleQR offers premium features via in-app subscription. Apple processes all payments securely, and we do not collect or store any payment information.

iCloud Storage: If you choose to save QR templates and logos, this data is stored securely in your personal iCloud account. We do not access or collect your iCloud data.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Apple (iCloud, Subscriptions): https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Your Choices

You can manage ad preferences through your device settings or by visiting Google Ads Settings: https://adssettings.google.com

Subscription and iCloud permissions can also be controlled in your iOS Settings.
